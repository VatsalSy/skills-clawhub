# Education in Milan

## Overview

Milan has strong university presence, diverse private and public school options, and a robust international education segment.

## University and Higher Education Strength

| Segment | Typical Strength |
|---------|------------------|
| Design and fashion | Globally recognized programs |
| Business and economics | Strong corporate links |
| Engineering and tech | Solid technical institutions |
| Arts and architecture | High-quality legacy programs |

## Schooling Paths for Families

| Path | Best For | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| Public schools | Long-term local integration | Language adaptation required |
| Private Italian schools | Structured private path | Varies by curriculum |
| International schools | Expat and mobile families | Higher cost and demand |

## Cost Reality

- Public pathways are cheaper but require language and admin adaptation.
- International schools are capacity-constrained and expensive.
- Commute can strongly influence school quality of life.

## Parent Decision Framework

1. Define language and curriculum goal first.
2. Map school route to daily commute.
3. Budget for full annual cost, not only tuition.
