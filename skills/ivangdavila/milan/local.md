# Local Services in Milan

## Essentials Setup

| Service | Typical Choice | Notes |
|---------|----------------|-------|
| Mobile SIM | TIM, Vodafone, WINDTRE, iliad | Price and coverage vary by package |
| Banking | Traditional bank or digital option | Documents drive approval speed |
| Payments | Card-first with cash backup | Small cash still useful |
| Delivery apps | Multiple options | Strong in central districts |
| Transport apps | ATM and rail apps | Useful for disruptions and planning |

## Practical Daily Infrastructure

- Pharmacies are dense and usually easy to access.
- Supermarkets vary from discount to premium by district.
- Sunday schedules differ by neighborhood and store type.

## Admin and Service Tips

1. Keep digital copies of key documents.
2. Confirm opening hours before visiting public offices.
3. Use appointment systems when possible.

## Shopping and Household Basics

- Home goods and electronics are easy to source citywide.
- Price differences can be large between central and outer zones.
- Delivery windows are often tighter in central areas.
