# Milan Visitor Itineraries

## 1-Day First Visit

### Morning
- Duomo and rooftop access.
- Coffee break in central area.

### Midday
- Walk through Galleria.
- Lunch in Brera or nearby side streets.

### Afternoon
- Pinacoteca di Brera or Castello Sforzesco.
- Short stop at Parco Sempione.

### Evening
- Navigli aperitivo and canal walk.

## 3-Day Balanced Itinerary

### Day 1: Historic Core
- Duomo, Galleria, Palazzo Reale area.
- Brera at night.

### Day 2: Art and Design
- Last Supper time slot.
- Triennale or Fondazione Prada.
- Dinner in Porta Romana or Isola.

### Day 3: Local Milan
- Morning market or neighborhood walk.
- San Siro or modern district (CityLife / Porta Nuova).
- Aperitivo plus late dinner.

| Day | Plan |

## Profile Variants

### Culture Heavy
Prioritize museums, architecture, and concerts at La Scala area.

### Family Friendly
Add parks, science museum, and shorter walking segments.

### Food Focused
Plan by neighborhoods: Brera, Navigli, Porta Romana, Isola.

### Football Focused
Anchor dates around match schedule and stadium transport.

## Timing Rules

- Keep 1 major ticketed activity per half day.
- Avoid cross-city zigzag in rush hours.
- Reserve at least one flexible block per day for weather or delays.
