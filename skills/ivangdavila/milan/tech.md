# Milan Tech Market Guide

## Market Position

Milan is Italy's top private-sector tech and digital jobs hub, with strongest density in fintech, e-commerce, B2B SaaS, and enterprise software.

## Compensation Ranges (Gross Annual)

| Role Level | Typical Range |
|------------|----------------|
| Junior software engineer | EUR 28,000-40,000 |
| Mid-level software engineer | EUR 40,000-60,000 |
| Senior software engineer | EUR 55,000-90,000 |
| Engineering manager | EUR 75,000-130,000 |
| Product manager | EUR 50,000-95,000 |

## Hiring Reality

- English-friendly roles exist but Italian often increases options.
- Hybrid models are common across larger companies.
- Interview speed varies heavily by company maturity.

## Best Districts for Tech Workers

- Porta Nuova and Isola for office density and networking.
- Bicocca for university and research-adjacent opportunities.
- CityLife and nearby corridors for enterprise and consulting.

## Career Strategy

1. Target firms by compensation band, not only brand name.
2. Clarify remote and hybrid expectations early.
3. Map commute before accepting offers tied to specific districts.
