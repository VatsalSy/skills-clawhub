# Best Food Areas in Milan

## Quick Area Match

| Goal | Best Area | Why |
|------|-----------|-----|
| Classic Milan dishes | Brera and central trattorie | Strong traditional density |
| Social aperitivo | Navigli | Canal-side concentration |
| Date-night modern dining | Porta Romana | High quality without full center crowds |
| Trendy food scene | Isola | New concepts and creative kitchens |
| Value and diversity | Chinatown and nearby streets | Strong range and pricing |
| Business dinners | Porta Nuova | Professional setting and access |

## Time-of-Day Strategy

- Lunch: business districts and local bars.
- Aperitivo: Navigli, Porta Venezia, Isola.
- Dinner: Brera, Porta Romana, Isola, select central pockets.

## Area Traps

- Tourist-centered menus near Duomo square.
- Peak-hour crowds without reservation in Navigli.
- Assuming all bars include substantial aperitivo food.

## High-Confidence Plan

1. Pick one area per meal block.
2. Reserve dinner first, then fill lunch around itinerary.
3. Keep one weather-proof indoor backup.
