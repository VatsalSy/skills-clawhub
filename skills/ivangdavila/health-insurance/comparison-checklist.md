## Final Recommendation Pattern

1. Identify the plan with best expected annual cost.
2. Compare downside risk under high-use scenario.
3. If tie, prefer lower administrative friction and better provider continuity.
4. Provide a one-line recommendation plus two quantified trade-offs.
