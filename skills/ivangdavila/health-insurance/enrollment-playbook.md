Use this execution sequence after plan selection.

## 1. Pre-Enrollment Package

Collect and verify:
- Candidate plan IDs and exact plan names
- Effective date and enrollment deadline
- Required documents for enrollment proof
- Support contact channels and escalation path

## 2. Enrollment Execution

- Submit enrollment through approved channel.
- Capture confirmation ID and timestamp.
- Save screenshots or confirmation artifacts.
- Verify the submitted plan matches the recommended plan exactly.

## 3. Post-Enrollment Verification

Within 48 hours, confirm:
- Enrollment status is active or pending with expected timeline.
- Primary providers are discoverable in network tools.
- Key medications remain covered under expected tier.

## 4. Renewal and Change Triggers

Create reminders for:
- Open enrollment start and end dates
- Mid-year qualifying life events
- Premium increase notifications
- Provider network changes affecting continuity

## 5. Dispute Preparedness

If billing or denial issues happen, preserve:
- Enrollment evidence
- Policy communication timeline
- Claim and denial identifiers
- Notes on promises made by support representatives
