# Useful Apps for Japan Travel

## Transport and Navigation

- **Google Maps** — baseline station routing and transfer timing
- **Japan Transit Planner tools** — better rail-platform detail in complex routes
- **Local metro apps** — useful for service alerts in major cities

## Booking and Dining

- **Tabelog and booking links** — useful for restaurant quality signals and reservations
- **Airline and rail operator apps** — direct disruption and seat updates

## Weather and Outdoors

- **Weather and typhoon-tracking apps** — important in summer and coastal periods
- **Offline maps** — essential for hiking and rural routes

## Practical

- **Translation apps** — useful for menus and signage outside key hubs
- **Payment apps/card tools** — useful for spending tracking and backup

## Workflow Tip

Use apps for discovery first, then verify directly with operators before paying for high-value bookings.
