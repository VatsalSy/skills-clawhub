## Tokyo

### Best zones
- **Shinjuku side streets:** dense choices with varied budget levels
- **Shibuya backstreets:** modern bar and club mix
- **Koenji and Shimokitazawa:** more local-feeling music and bar culture

## Osaka

### Best zones
- **Namba edge:** broad nightlife access with heavy crowds
- **Tenma and Ura-Namba:** better value and local pacing

## Kyoto and Other Cities

### Best zones
- Kyoto side streets near Gion and Kiyamachi for lower-intensity evening routes
- Hiroshima compact center for manageable one-night circuits

## Practical Notes

- Last-train timing matters for route safety and cost.
- High-demand bars may need reservations even on weekdays.
- Late-night food options vary by neighborhood.

## Mistakes to Avoid

1. Assuming all nightlife starts and ends at same times by city.
2. Staying only in highest-tourist strips.
3. No transport plan before final venue.
