### Namba and Dotonbori edge
**Vibe:** Neon-heavy food and nightlife, high tourist volume.
**Best for:** One concentrated evening, then branch out.

### Umeda and Osaka Station side
**Vibe:** Modern city core, transport convenience, shopping density.
**Best for:** Practical base and multi-direction day routes.

### Tenma and Nakazakicho
**Vibe:** Local bars, izakaya alleys, better value atmosphere.
**Best for:** Food and nightlife beyond the busiest strips.

### Shinsekai and south pockets
**Vibe:** Classic retro energy with variable quality by block.
**Best for:** Short focused visits, not all-evening commitment.

## Osaka Strategy

- Prioritize food neighborhoods over attraction-only routing.
- Use Osaka as a practical base for Kansai day trips.
- Keep one flexible slot for late-night recovery.

## Mistakes to Avoid

1. Spending every night only in Dotonbori.
2. Ignoring reservation needs on peak weekends.
3. Stacking heavy food crawls without pacing.
