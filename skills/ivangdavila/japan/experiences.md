# Signature Experiences in Japan

## Urban Experiences

- Tokyo neighborhood and market split day with evening izakaya route
- Kyoto temple morning plus craft and tea-focused afternoon
- Osaka food crawl with local bar district finish

## Culture and Nature Experiences

- Shrine and temple circuits with early start strategy
- Onsen and ryokan overnights with etiquette-aware planning
- Mountain or coastal day routes with weather flexibility

## Seasonal Experiences

| Season | Good choices |
|--------|--------------|
| Spring | Blossom routes and balanced city exploration |
| Summer | Festival windows with heat-aware pacing |
| Autumn | Foliage and cultural routes with strong atmosphere |
| Winter | Snow regions, onsen focus, and city-food value |

## Mistakes to Avoid

1. Treating one fixed plan as weather-proof.
2. Overfilling days with transfers plus late nights.
3. Ignoring season-specific crowd spikes.
