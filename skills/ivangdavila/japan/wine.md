## Main Beverage Themes

| Category | What to Expect |
|----------|----------------|
| Japanese wine | Smaller-scale regions with selective high-quality producers |
| Sake | Strong regional identity and style variation |
| Japanese whisky | High demand, variable availability, premium pricing |
| Shochu and local spirits | Region-specific value beyond big-city cocktail menus |

## Route and Planning Tips

- Keep beverage-heavy plans on non-driving days.
- Book distillery or brewery tours in advance where required.
- Pair tastings with structured meal breaks.

## City Pairings

### Tokyo
Strongest concentration of curated bars and premium tasting programs.

### Kyoto and Fushimi area
Useful for sake-focused routes with short transfers.

### Osaka
Great balance of casual and higher-end whisky and sake bars.

## Mistakes to Avoid

1. Overpacking tastings in one evening.
2. Assuming every famous bar accepts walk-ins.
3. Driving after multi-stop beverage routes.
4. Treating all "Japanese whisky" menus as equal quality.
