# Shanghai Visa and Residence Guide (2026)

Use this as a practical orientation, not legal advice.
China immigration rules can update with limited notice.

## Common visa routes

| Route | Typical purpose | Work allowed |
|---|---|---|
| L visa | Tourism, short personal travel | No |
| M visa | Business visits, meetings | No salaried local employment |
| Z visa + permit | Formal employment | Yes |
| X1/X2 visa | Study | Limited, school and permit dependent |
| S/Q family routes | Family reunion/visit | Usually no direct employment |

## Work route in practice

| Step | Typical owner | Typical timeline |
|---|---|---|
| Employer obtains work authorization notice | Employer | About 1-4 weeks |
| Applicant gets Z visa abroad | Applicant | About 4-10 business days |
| Entry to China and medical check | Applicant | First 1-2 weeks |
| Work permit card + residence permit | Employer + applicant | About 2-6 weeks |

## Core documents often requested

- Passport with sufficient validity and blank pages.
- Employer documents and signed contract.
- Education and experience proof (authenticated when required).
- Criminal record check (jurisdiction dependent).
- Medical exam records from designated channels.
- Shanghai address registration record.

## Family/dependent planning

| Topic | Practical note |
|---|---|
| Spouse and children timing | Often easier after main applicant permit is active |
| School enrollment | Start early; paperwork can be slow |
| Insurance | Keep bridge coverage during transitions |

## Common delays

| Cause | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Incomplete authentication chain | Prepare notarization/apostille sequence early |
| Mismatch in name formatting | Keep exact passport spelling everywhere |
| Expiring passport | Renew before filing if validity is low |
| Housing registration gaps | Re-register after each move |

## Compliance habits

1. Track permit expiry dates 90, 60, and 30 days out.
2. Update registration promptly after address changes.
3. Keep digital and paper copies of all approvals.
4. Confirm employer HR process owner for every renewal.

## 2026 practical note

For current rules, verify with the relevant Chinese consulate and Shanghai Exit-Entry Administration before filing.
