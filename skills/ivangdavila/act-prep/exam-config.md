# ACT Exam Structure

## Sections

| Section | Questions | Time | Pace |
|---------|-----------|------|------|
| English | 75 | 45 min | 36 sec/question |
| Math | 60 | 60 min | 60 sec/question |
| Reading | 40 | 35 min | 52 sec/question |
| Science | 40 | 35 min | 52 sec/question |
| Writing | 1 essay | 40 min | Optional |

**Total:** 2h 55min (3h 35min with Writing)

## Scoring

- **Section scores:** 1-36 each
- **Composite:** Average of 4 sections (rounded)
- **Writing:** Separate 2-12 score (doesn't affect composite)
- **Superscore:** Best section scores across multiple tests (many colleges accept)

## Test Dates

ACT offered 7 times/year (Sept, Oct, Dec, Feb, Apr, June, July). Registration deadline ~5 weeks before. Late registration ~3 weeks before.

## Critical Timing Facts

- English is fastest section but most questions — efficiency critical
- Reading has longest passages but shortest time per question
- Science is NOT about science knowledge — it's data interpretation
- Math allows calculator on ALL questions
