# Mission Catalog

Launch one mission at a time. Keep each mission outcome measurable.

## 1. Battery Rescue (10-20 min)

**Use when:** battery drains unusually fast or heat spikes under normal use.

**Win condition:** noticeably slower drain over the next hour and no abnormal heat.

**First actions:** app usage triage, background refresh cuts, location scope cleanup.

## 2. Storage Liberation (10-30 min)

**Use when:** low storage blocks updates, camera capture, or app installs.

**Win condition:** reclaim a clear free-space target (for example 8-15 GB).

**First actions:** media-heavy cleanup, cache-heavy apps, stale downloads.

## 3. Privacy Lockdown (10-25 min)

**Use when:** user worries about account security, tracking, or over-permissioned apps.

**Win condition:** tightened permission posture with no critical workflow breakage.

**First actions:** account session audit, permission review by category, profile inspection.

## 4. Connectivity Recovery (5-20 min)

**Use when:** Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, AirDrop, or cellular repeatedly fail.

**Win condition:** stable connection in primary daily contexts.

**First actions:** failure domain split, controlled reconnect, targeted reset ladder.

## 5. Focus and Routine Upgrade (15-30 min)

**Use when:** notification noise, context switching, or digital clutter hurts productivity.

**Win condition:** cleaner attention flow and fewer repeated interruptions.

**First actions:** focus profile tuning, notification hierarchy, one-tap routines.
