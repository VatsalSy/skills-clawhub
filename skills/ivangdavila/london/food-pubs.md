## Pub Etiquette

| Rule | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Order at bar** | Table service rare except gastropubs |
| **Tipping** | Not expected for drinks |
| **Rounds** | Take turns buying for the group |
| **Last orders** | Called 10-15 min before closing (~11pm) |
| **Food** | Usually lunch 12-3, dinner 6-9 |

---

## Types of Pubs

| Type | What to Expect |
|------|----------------|
| **Traditional** | Ale, cask beer, basic pub grub |
| **Gastropub** | Restaurant-quality food, booking advised |
| **Craft beer** | Rotating taps, modern vibe |
| **Historic** | Character, often riverside |
