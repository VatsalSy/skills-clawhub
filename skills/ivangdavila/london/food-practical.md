# Food Practical Guide — London

## Dietary Needs

### Vegetarian & Vegan

London is excellent for plant-based dining.

| Restaurant | Type | Location | Price |
|------------|------|----------|-------|
| **Plates** | Vegan fine dining (Michelin ⭐) | Shoreditch | £££ |
| **Mildred's** | Vegetarian institution | Multiple | ££ |
| **Holy Carrot** | Upscale vegan | Knightsbridge | £££ |
| **Facing Heaven** | Vegan Sichuan | Hackney | ££ |
| **Gauthier Soho** | Vegan fine dining | Soho | £££ |

**Chains:** Wagamama, Honest Burgers, Leon have good vegan options.

Most mid-range+ restaurants accommodate GF. Good chains: Nando's, Wagamama, Leon.

## Delivery Apps

| App | Strengths | Subscription |
|-----|-----------|--------------|
| **Deliveroo** | Best restaurant selection | Plus £3.49/mo |
| **Uber Eats** | Good coverage, groceries | Uber One £7.99/mo |
| **Just Eat** | Widest coverage | None |

**Tips:**
- Compare prices — same restaurant differs by app
- Check restaurant's own site (cheaper, keeps more revenue)
- Collection discount often 10-20% off
- Friday/Saturday dinner = long waits, surcharges

## Practical Tips

### Reservations

- **Needed for:** Popular restaurants, weekend brunch, Sunday roast
- **Apps:** OpenTable, Resy, SevenRooms
- **Walk-ins work:** Markets, pubs, casual places, weekday lunches

### Tipping

| Where | How Much |
|-------|----------|
| **Restaurants** | 10-15% (often added as "service charge") |
| **Pubs/Bars** | Not expected |
| **Delivery** | £1-2 optional |

### Money-Saving

| Tip | Details |
|-----|---------|
| **Set menus** | Always cheaper than à la carte |
| **Lunch deals** | Upscale restaurants do cheaper set lunch (12-2:30) |
| **Pre-theatre** | West End deals 5-6:30pm |
| **BYO** | Some places (Kaosarn, curry houses) allow wine |
| **Market grazing** | Cheaper than restaurants |
| **Supermarket deals** | Tesco, Sainsbury's, M&S do £3-5 lunch deals |

### Best Times

| When | What |
|------|------|
| **Lunch** | 12-2:30 for set menu deals |
| **Pre-theatre** | 5-6:30pm for West End area deals |
| **Markets** | Before 11am to avoid queues |
| **Brunch** | Book ahead Sat-Sun 10am-2pm |
