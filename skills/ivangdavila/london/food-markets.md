# Food Markets — London

## Borough Market

**Location:** London Bridge | **When:** Wed-Sat (full), limited Mon-Tue

**Best for:** Fresh produce, cheese, street food lunch

**Must-try:**
- **Padella** — Fresh pasta (queue early)
- **Kappacasein** — Raclette, toasted cheese
- **Brindisa** — Spanish chorizo sandwiches
- **Bread Ahead** — Doughnuts
- **Monmouth Coffee** — Famous roaster
- **Neal's Yard Dairy** — British artisan cheese

**Tip:** Very crowded Saturdays. Friday morning ideal.

---

## Maltby Street Market

**Location:** Bermondsey (railway arches) | **When:** Sat 10-5, Sun 11-4

**Best for:** Less touristy Borough alternative, wine bars

**Must-try:**
- **Comptoir Gourmand** — French pastries
- **40 Maltby Street** — Natural wine bar
- **Beefsteaks** — Steak sandwiches
- **Little Bird Gin** — Gin tastings

---

## Broadway Market

**Location:** Hackney | **When:** Saturday 9-5

**Best for:** East London vibes, brunch, vintage alongside food

**Must-try:**
- **F. Cooke** — Pie & mash
- **L'Eau à la Bouche** — French pastries
- Street food stalls rotate weekly

---

## Camden Market

**Location:** Camden | **When:** Daily 10-late

**Best for:** International street food, atmosphere

**Highlights:** Global cuisines, Caribbean, Asian fusion

**Note:** Very touristy but genuinely good variety
