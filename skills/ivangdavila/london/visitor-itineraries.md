# London Itineraries

## One Day (First-Timer)

| Time | Activity | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| 9am | Tower of London | Go straight to Crown Jewels |
| 12pm | Borough Market | Lunch, don't over-buy |
| 2pm | Walk South Bank | Tate Modern, Shakespeare's Globe views |
| 4pm | Cross Millennium Bridge | St Paul's views |
| 5pm | Covent Garden | Street performers, coffee |
| 7pm | West End show or dinner Soho | Book show ahead |

**Transport:** Walk + Tube. Buy Oyster or use contactless.

## Seven Days (Deep Dive)

**Days 1-3:** Essential itinerary above

### Day 4: Museums
- Natural History Museum or V&A (pick one)
- Science Museum if with kids
- Afternoon: Kensington area, Hyde Park
- Evening: Notting Hill dinner

### Day 5: North London
- Morning: Hampstead Heath walk
- Lunch: Hampstead village
- Afternoon: Camden Market
- Evening: Islington dinner + comedy

### Day 6: East London
- Morning: Columbia Road Market (Sun only)
- Lunch: Hackney brunch spot
- Afternoon: Victoria Park, street art walk
- Evening: Shoreditch nightlife

### Day 7: Day Trip or Chill
**Options:**
- Windsor Castle (train, 1 hour)
- Kew Gardens
- Hampton Court Palace
- Sleep in, explore favorite area
