# Food Overview — London

London was named the world's best food city. Its strength lies in extraordinary diversity — from Michelin-starred temples to £3 bagels, authentic Indian to experimental vegan.

## What London Does Well

| Cuisine | Why | Best Areas |
|---------|-----|------------|
| **Indian/South Asian** | Historic immigration | Brick Lane, Tooting, Southall |
| **Middle Eastern** | Lebanese, Turkish, Persian | Edgware Road, Green Lanes |
| **Chinese** | Cantonese dim sum | Chinatown, Deptford |
| **Modern British** | Farm-to-table | Shoreditch, Clerkenwell |
| **Italian** | Fresh pasta specialists | Soho, Borough Market |
| **Markets** | Global cuisines | Borough, Maltby Street |

**Key insight:** London's strength is having world-class versions of *everything* within tube distance.
