# Visitor Tips — London

## Transport Basics

### Payment
- **Contactless** (phone/card) = Oyster prices
- No need to buy Oyster anymore
- Daily cap: £8.60 (Zones 1-2)

### Best Apps
- **Citymapper** — Essential for navigation
- **TfL Go** — Official, good for disruptions
- **Trainline** — Day trips

### Tips
- Walk more than you think — many sights close together
- Avoid tube at rush hour (8-9am, 5-6:30pm)
- Bus #11, #24 go past landmarks (top deck, front)
- Thames Clipper boats: scenic + useful (Oyster works)

## Weather Reality

- **Pack:** Layers + waterproof jacket
- **Reality:** Less rain than NYC, just drizzle
- **Summer:** Actually warm (July ~24°C)
- **Winter:** Grey but mild (rarely freezing)

---

## Safety Quick Guide

- **Main risk:** Pickpocketing (not violence)
- **Watch:** Oxford St, tube, Westminster Bridge
- **Phone:** Front pocket, not back
- **Bag:** Cross-body, zip facing you
- **Scams:** Ignore clipboard charity, free CDs

**Emergency:** 999 | Non-emergency police: 101
