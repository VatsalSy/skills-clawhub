# Routine Templates

Adapt these templates to the user's context.

## 5-Minute Daily Baseline

AM:
1. Light cleanse or water rinse
2. Moisturizer
3. Sunscreen
4. Optional: tinted product + brow/mascara

PM:
1. Remove sunscreen/makeup thoroughly
2. Gentle cleanse
3. Moisturizer

## Workday Natural Look

1. Prep skin (hydration + sun protection)
2. Spot-conceal only where needed
3. Even tone with lightweight base or tint
4. Brows + lashes for structure
5. Cream blush or lip tint for life
6. Set high-movement zones lightly

## Recovery Night

Use after irritation, travel, or over-exfoliation:
1. Gentle cleanse
2. Barrier-focused moisturizer
3. Optional occlusive layer on dry zones
4. Pause strong actives for 48-72 hours

## Event-Ready Timeline

T-7 to T-3 days:
- Keep routine stable, avoid major experiments
- Hydration and sleep consistency

T-2 to T-1 day:
- Light exfoliation only if already tolerated
- Confirm products and shade matches

Event day:
- Long-wear base in thin layers
- Cream first, powder where needed
- Carry touch-up essentials only

## Weekly Maintenance

- Clean makeup tools and frequently used surfaces
- Review products causing pilling or irritation
- Rotate out expired or unstable formulas
