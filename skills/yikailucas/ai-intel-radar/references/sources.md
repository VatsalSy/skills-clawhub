# AI Intel Radar Sources

## Recommended source tiers

### Tier 1 (primary / official)
- OpenAI blog: https://openai.com/news
- Anthropic news: https://www.anthropic.com/news
- Google DeepMind blog: https://deepmind.google/discover/blog/
- Meta AI blog: https://ai.meta.com/blog/
- Microsoft AI blog: https://blogs.microsoft.com/ai/

### Tier 2 (technical depth)
- arXiv AI categories: https://arxiv.org/list/cs.AI/recent
- arXiv CL: https://arxiv.org/list/cs.CL/recent
- arXiv ML: https://arxiv.org/list/cs.LG/recent
- Papers with Code: https://paperswithcode.com/
- Hugging Face blog: https://huggingface.co/blog

### Tier 3 (market and product signals)
- a16z AI: https://a16z.com/category/artificial-intelligence/
- Sequoia insights: https://www.sequoiacap.com/article/
- YC blog: https://www.ycombinator.com/blog
- Stratechery: https://stratechery.com/

### Chinese channels (cross-check)
- 机器之心: https://www.jiqizhixin.com/
- 量子位: https://www.qbitai.com/
- 新智元: https://www.ai-start.com/
- InfoQ 中文: https://www.infoq.cn/

## Selection heuristics

1. Prefer primary source > secondary commentary.
2. Keep updates from last 24h (daily) or 7 days (weekly).
3. Remove duplicates on same event.
4. Include at least one source URL per item.
5. Flag uncertain info with "待验证".
