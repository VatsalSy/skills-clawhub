# Deal Desk — Structured Deal Review & Approval

Stop leaking margin on every custom deal. This skill gives your AI agent a complete deal desk framework: discount guardrails, approval workflows, margin analysis, and deal structure templates.

## What It Does
- Reviews non-standard deals against pricing guardrails
- Calculates margin impact, LTV:CAC, and payback period
- Routes approvals based on discount level
- Provides "give to get" offset strategies (never discount for free)
- Flags 7 deal-killing red flags before they cost you
- Includes 4 deal structure templates (standard, multi-year, enterprise, partner)
- Industry-specific deal patterns for 10 verticals

## Who It's For
- Revenue leaders tired of ad-hoc pricing decisions
- Sales teams that need faster deal approvals
- Finance teams tracking margin erosion
- Anyone closing B2B deals above $10K ACV
