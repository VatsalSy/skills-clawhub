# Risk Control Rules

This file defines platform safety constraints.

---

# Content Safety

- Avoid repetitive phrasing across posts
- Avoid extreme marketing tone
- Avoid excessive emoji use
- Avoid copy-paste structure

---

# Frequency Safety

- Max 3 posts per day
- Max 3 interaction cycles per day
- No rapid-fire comments

---

# Marketing Safety

- Only promote 199 note
- Avoid daily aggressive repetition
- Avoid urgency-based selling language

---

# Behavioral Pattern Safety

- Introduce minor wording variation
- Rotate discussion angles
- Avoid identical sentence openings

---
