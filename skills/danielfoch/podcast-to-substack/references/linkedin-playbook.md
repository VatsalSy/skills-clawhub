# LinkedIn Playbook

## Objective
Repurpose each Substack post to LinkedIn with a concise, data-first summary and link back to Substack.

## Format
- 1-line hook
- 3 bullet points with concrete numbers
- Link to Substack post
- Optional Apple Podcasts link
- One discussion question
- 4-6 relevant hashtags

## Guardrails
- Keep under ~1,800 chars.
- Avoid overhype language.
- Use plain UTF-8 bullets or dashes.
- Mention source/report when quoting numbers.
